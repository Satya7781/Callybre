Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 16, 2026

At Callybre, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or purchase our products.

1 Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you make a purchase, create an account, or contact us, we collect your name, email address, billing and shipping addresses, and phone number.

Payment Information

We collect your credit card details or payment information; however, this is processed securely by our third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay) and we do not store your full credit card number on our servers.

Automated Data

We automatically collect your IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior on our site using cookies and similar tracking technologies.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and returns.
  • Communicate with you regarding your order, customer service inquiries, and warranty claims.
  • Send you promotional emails and newsletters (only if you have opted in).
  • Improve our website, products, and customer experience.
  • Prevent fraud and enhance the security of our website.

3 Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our business (e.g., shipping carriers like FedEx/UPS, payment processors, email marketing platforms).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to understand how you use our site and to improve your experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of our site may not function properly without them.

5 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (including SSL encryption) to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6 Your Data Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., under GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at business@callybre.com.
